# Download Netperf Download copies of netperf and other benchmarks from one of the following sources: * [ftp.netperf.org](ftp://ftp.netperf.org/netperf/)</A> contains tar/zip files of released sources. * [subversion repository](http://www.netperf.org/svn/netperf2/tags/) for tagged releases * [The very latest netperf bits](http://www.netperf.org/svn/netperf2/trunk/) from subversion You may also find some places with copies or mirrors by searching at the search engine of your choice.
